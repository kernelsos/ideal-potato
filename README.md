# About 
This is a Next.js app where creators can create streams, and their fans can visit the platform to vote for the songs they want played during the stream. Users can also downvote other songs. 


# Further improvements:
1. Adding a pay-to-interrupt system where users can pay money, and the currently playing song will be interrupted, and the user who paid the money will be able to play the specific song. The money will go to the creator. 

2. Adding an event-based trigger to automatically play the next song when the currently playing song ends.
